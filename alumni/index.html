Alumni

Dr. Long Wang

PostDoc - 2020.10 to 2023.10

Phosphorus is an essential plant nutrient, which participates in all metabolic processes in plants. Due to the easy fixation by soil, nearly 70% of global arable land suffers from Pi deficiency. Thus, farmers have to apply more phosphate fertilizer to guarantee the plant growth and crop yield, which in turn also causes severe eutrophication. How to solve these problems? We first studied the mechanism of phosphorus absorption, transportation, storage and utilization by plants, and we also paid attention to the absorption and utilization of phosphorus by algae.
